Their Story

Waleed and Emma lived a block from each other for their last two years at the University of Michigan. Unfortunately, they did not meet until a month before they graduated. This would not have been such a big deal, had Waleed not committed to moving to Chicago just a month after graduating. Having been together for only two months, these crazy kids decided to make the whole long distant thing work. And they did. They were apart for a year visiting each other every other weekend or more. A year after Waleed left for the windy city, Emma joined him, and that same summer, Waleed made the easy decision to get down on one knee and ask the love of his life to spend the rest of it with him. She said yes.
